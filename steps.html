Steps


1. Grab materials


Bread, Cheese, Butter


2. Get the stove ready


Put the bread first, then cheese, and another piece of bread


3. Put it on the stove


Put the bread first, then cheese, and another piece of bread


4. Butter


Spread the butter on the bread then flip


5. Wait


Until the bread is not too crispy on the side wiith the butter


6. Repeat


Repeats steps 4-5 for the other side


7. Eat


Take it off the stove, and cut it into TRIANGLES NOT RECTANGLES!
